What is Text Encryption and Decryption?

Text Encryption is the process of converting plain text into unreadable ciphertext using an algorithm and a key. This ensures that unauthorized individuals cannot access your sensitive information.

Text Decryption is the reverse process where the ciphertext is converted back to its original plain text using the appropriate key.
